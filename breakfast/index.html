Breakfast

Poha_____________________199 rs

Flattened rice (poha), mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, green chilies, onions, turmeric powder, salt, lemon juice, and garnished with coriander leaves.

Masala Dosa__________________________120 rs

Fermented rice and urad dal batter, potatoes, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, green chilies, onions, turmeric powder, asafoetida (hing), salt, and stuffed with a spiced potato filling, served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Upma_________________________100 rs

Semolina (rava/sooji), mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal, curry leaves, green chilies, onions, ginger, vegetables (optional), turmeric powder, salt, and garnished with coriander leaves.

Rava Dhokla__________________________75 rs

Semolina (rava/sooji), yogurt, water, green chilies, ginger, eno (fruit salt), salt, turmeric powder, mustard seeds, sesame seeds, and garnished with coriander leaves.

Aloo paratha__________________________120 rs

Wheat flour, potatoes, ghee, green chilies, ginger, coriander leaves, cumin powder, red chili powder, turmeric powder, salt, and served with yogurt or pickle.

Idli__________________________65 rs

Fermented rice and urad dal batter, salt, and steamed to make soft, spongy rice cakes.

Poori__________________________100 rs

Wheat flour, water, salt, and oil, deep-fried to make puffed and fluffy Indian bread.

Chole Bhature__________________________100 rs

Chickpeas, tomatoes, onions, spices (for chole); All-purpose flour, yogurt, and oil (for bhature).

Akki Roti__________________________100 rs

Rice flour, water, salt, finely chopped onions, green chilies, coriander leaves, and sometimes grated coconut, mixed to form a dough, and then flattened and cooked on a griddle until golden brown.

Uttapam__________________________100 rs

Rice and urad dal batter, finely chopped onions, tomatoes, green chilies, and cilantro, seasoned with salt and cooked on a griddle until crispy.

Puri Bhaji__________________________100 rs

Wheat flour, water, salt (for puri), and potatoes, onions, tomatoes, spices (such as cumin, turmeric, and garam masala) for the spiced potato curry (bhaji).
